SV449-2

The CS Series line are individually vehicle load specific optimized wheels via FEA Simulation Tests. This results in the lightest, best performing, and strongest wheel that can possibly be made for your car. The Competition Spec Series come standard with spoke cut-outs and have upgradable options such as titanium hardware and backpad pocketing for further weight reduction.

  • ONE PIECE FORGED MONOBLOCK
  • AVAILABLE IN DIAMETERS 18.0-24.0 INCH
  • WIDTHS RANGE FROM 8.5-13.5+ INCH
  • AVAILABLE FOR ANY VEHICLE APPLICATION
